Drip Coffee

Drip coffee is brewed by pouring hot water over ground coffee beans, allowing it to slowly filter through a paper or metal filter.

Syphon Coffee

Syphon coffee is brewed using a vacuum-based system where water is heated, forced upward, then filtered back down through coffee grounds.

French Press Coffee

French Press Coffee is a method where coarsely ground coffee is steeped in hot water, then separated by pressing a plunger.
